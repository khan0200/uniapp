How to Check Visa Status
1
Fill in the form above
Enter passport number, birth date, and English name
2
Click "Open Korea Visa Portal"
This will open the official visa.go.kr website
3
On the visa portal:
• Select "재외공관" (Overseas Embassy)
• Select "여권번호" (Passport Number) from dropdown
• Paste/enter the information
• Click "Search"
4
Check the result
Look for "진행상태" (Status) in the results
Status Meanings:
접수 (Received/Applied) - Application received, waiting for processing
허가 (Approved) - Visa approved!
불허 (Rejected) - Visa rejected
